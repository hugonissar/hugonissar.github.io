This is the start of a new home for my open-source MarTech work. The two
projects I'm currently maintaining are:

- **[BigQuery-Read-Only-MCP-Server](/projects/bigquery-readonly-mcp-server/)**
  — an MCP server for connecting AI agents (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor) to
  Google BigQuery, with hard table allowlists and per-query scan caps.
- **[GA4-Ecommerce-BQML-Purchase-Propensity](/projects/ga4-ecommerce-bqml-purchase-propensity/)**
  — a complete BQML pipeline that predicts purchase propensity from GA4
  events and pushes the result back to GA4 for Google Ads remarketing.

Posts here will be one of three things, usually in order of how often:

1. **Walkthroughs** of a specific piece of MarTech infrastructure I've built
   or maintained — what it does, why it's designed the way it is, and how to
   deploy it.
2. **Comparisons** of approaches: managed vs. self-hosted, BQML vs. Vertex,
   Measurement Protocol vs. Ads API, that kind of thing.
3. **Short notes** on something I learned the hard way and would have liked
   someone to write down for me first.

All code is MIT-licensed. Everything on this site is open. If you want updates,
the [RSS feed](/feed.xml) is the most reliable channel.
